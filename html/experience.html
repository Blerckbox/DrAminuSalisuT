Experience

Dr Aminu Salisu Tsauri, supervised over one hundred undergraduate projects and more than fifty postgraduate dissertations, published over forty scholarly articles in reputable journals, and participated in over fifty academic conferences locally and internationally.

He also served as Level Adviser, Postgraduate Coordinator, and Pioneer Deputy Director, School of Continuing Education.

In the realm of political service, He remained a dedicated and loyal member of the All Progressives Congress (APC), contributing actively to the success of the present administration. He served as Director General, Katsinawan Dikko Ga Dikko Movement; Secretary, Directorate for Youth Mobilisation, Tinubu/Dikko Campaign Council, Katsina State; Chairman, Inaugural Lecture Committee for the Swearing-in of the Current Administration; and Member, Publicity Committee for the Governor and Deputy’s Inauguration Ceremony.

He is passionate about community empowerment and education reform. He served as Chairman, Tudun Matawalle Community Development Association, Katsina, and as a member of the Tsauri Development Association, where they promoted peacebuilding, youth inclusion, and rural transformation.
